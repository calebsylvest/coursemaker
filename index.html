A new way to build

Managing a website is complex. Popular CMS platforms like WordPress, Drupal, and Expression Engine offer great features and extensions, but at the expense of ease-of-use, simplicity, and cost.

No Code

The Helix editor requires zero knowledge of coding languages to use, so anyone will feel comfortable modifying content.

Sheer Speed

Save time and money in development by building for the web like we always have, and forget the bloated CMS platforms.

HTML + CSS + JS

Helix does not necessitate how a site should be built, instead it functions as a management add-on.

Edit Anywhere

Helix works from home or on the go, with a computer, tablet, or mobile device. We know content management is a must in many situations.

WYSIWYG

But with a good connotation. Traditional content management systems create a disconnect between the editor and what people see. Helix removes the disconnect.

Bold Italics Underline

Edit and style content from the front-end, because some things in life should be simple!

The technology stack

Helix is a Content Management System, but without the system. Helix offers a supplemental package that sits on top of the codebase, allowing content management without the bulky platform.

Simple. Smart. Solution.

  • Simple Installation

    Helix is simple to setup and get running. Simply connect your site to the Helix client using FTP information and you will be good-to-go.

  • Smart & Helpful

    Helix offers an excellent experience for simply managing website content without dealing with complex systems or code.

  • The Solution

    Finally developers have a way to build custom sites as they would like without focusing on complicated platforms, but still offer a management option.
